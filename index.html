About Oyulu

Introducing Oyulu, the innovative solution that merges the best of both worlds: comfortable, affordable accommodations and eco-friendly mobility. Oyulu offers a unique platform that connects travelers with cozy lodging options while providing access to Yulu's sustainable electric bikes and scooters.
Seamless Booking Experience: Users can easily find and book charming accommodations, ranging from private rooms to shared spaces, all in one app. Our intuitive interface allows for quick comparisons and instant bookings.
Eco-Friendly Transportation: Once you arrive, explore your destination effortlessly with Yulu’s fleet of electric bikes and scooters available for rent directly through the Oyulu app. Travel sustainably while enjoying the sights and sounds of the city.
Personalized Recommendations: Oyulu leverages smart algorithms to suggest lodging and transportation options tailored to your preferences, making every trip unique.
Integrated User Experience: Manage your entire travel itinerary in one place. From accommodation to transportation, Oyulu ensures a hassle-free experience with integrated booking confirmations and easy navigation.
Local Insights and Community Engagement: Engage with local hosts and fellow travelers through our community features, fostering connections and discovering hidden gems in every destination.
With Oyulu, enjoy a smarter, greener, and more connected travel experience. Embrace the journey, whether you're unwinding in a cozy home or zipping around on a Yulu bike—Oyulu has you covered.
